CSK

The Chennai Super Kings (CSK) are a premier franchise in the Indian Premier League (IPL), established in 2008 and based in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. Known for their immense consistency, they have won five IPL titles (2010, 2011, 2018, 2021, 2023), sharing the record for most wins with the Mumbai Indians. Led for most of their history by MS Dhoni and captained in 2024/2025 by Ruturaj Gaikwad under coach Stephen Fleming, they play their home matches at the M.A. Chidambaram Stadium.
